# Room 00097 — MASH

## What it is
The classic childhood fortune-telling game. Fill in 4 options per category, reveal your future.

## Mechanic
Categories: Partner, Job, City, Kids count. Plus the classic MASH home types. System picks a magic number (3-8) and eliminates every Nth option round-robin across all groups until one remains per category. Winners highlighted, losers struck through. Fortune displayed as a paragraph.
