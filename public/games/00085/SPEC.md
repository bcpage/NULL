# Room 00085 — Something Under the Bed

## What it is
It is 3am. Two questions. A timer on each. You probably won't sleep.

## Mechanic
Sequential questions appear with a countdown timer bar. Player picks an answer before time runs out — or the room picks for them. Each choice reveals a short outcome. After both questions, the room goes dark and delivers a quiet ending. No reset. No replay.

## Notes
- Pure client-side — no server logic, no persistence
- Timer expiry forces a random answer — the room doesn't wait for you
- Outcome text is deliberately quiet and unresolved
- Room goes dark at the end (background deepens)
