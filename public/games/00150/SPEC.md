# Room 00150 — TARDIS

## What it is
A blue police box exterior. Click to enter. Inside: much larger than outside. Animated interior with receding corridors, hexagonal console, time rotor. Six console buttons: Dematerialize (fails, stuck on police box), Scan (returns room count and compression ratio), Coordinates, Temporal Drive status, Chameleon Circuit (stuck), Exit.

## Navigation
- data-nav: matrix
- Connections: 00149 ← → 00151

## Notes
- Fully client-side — no server, no persistence
- Exterior: 200×280 canvas, pulsing lantern light, stepped roof, TARDIS blue gradient, 4-pane windows
- Interior: 400×240 canvas, receding corridor perspective illusion (looping depth), hexagonal central console
- Time rotor: glowing cylinder on top of center column, pulses up and down
- Ceiling roundels: 12 decorative circles orbiting center
- Enter transition: exterior fades out (0.8s), interior appears
- Console readout: text panel below console; each button populates it
- Compression ratio: 14,246.7 m³ interior ÷ 0.005 m³ exterior = 2,849,340:1
- Chameleon circuit always stuck on POLICE BOX; 116 repair attempts
- Note: "Bigger on the inside. This is not a metaphor."
