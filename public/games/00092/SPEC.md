# Room 00092 — Cheshire Cat

## What it is
The Cheshire Cat. It fades in and out. The smile is always last to disappear.

## Mechanic
ASCII cat + quote cycle. Every 6–12 seconds, the cat and quote fade out. The smile lingers 2 seconds longer than everything else. Then a new quote fades in with the cat, starting with the smile. Moving the mouse makes the cat fade slightly. Quotes are classic Carroll lines.
