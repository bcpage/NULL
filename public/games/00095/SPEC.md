# Room 00095 — The Yellow Door

## What it is
A yellow door. You can knock. You can open it. Nothing is behind it.

## Mechanic
Click the door to knock. After a few knocks it may swing open (CSS 3D perspective transform). Click again to close. Each knock/open/close shows a short message. Knock messages escalate in strangeness. What's behind it changes each time.
